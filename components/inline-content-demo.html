üìã New Content Types

Click on the active (highlighted) item to display inline content:

  • data-audio="url" - Play audio inline
  • data-video="url" - Play video inline
  • data-email="address" - Show email form
  • data-phone="number" - Show phone dialer
  • data-map="address" - Show Google Maps
  • data-iframe="url" - Embed any URL
  • data-autoplay - Auto-play audio/video (optional)

Note: Content types take priority over href navigation.

üé® Advanced Usage

Combining with href

If both content attributes and href are present, the content will be shown inline. The href is ignored. 

<!-- This will show video inline, NOT navigate -->
<lz-item 
    label="Tutorial" 
    data-video="tutorial.mp4"
    href="/tutorials">
</lz-item>

Multiple Content Types

You can mix different content types in the same dial:

<lz-dial>
    <lz-item label="Music" data-audio="song.mp3"></lz-item>
    <lz-item label="Video" data-video="clip.mp4"></lz-item>
    <lz-item label="Email" data-email="hi@example.com"></lz-item>
    <lz-item label="Call" data-phone="+1-555-0123"></lz-item>
    <lz-item label="Map" data-map="Central Park, NY"></lz-item>
    <lz-item label="Home" href="/"></lz-item> <!-- Regular link -->
</lz-dial>

Autoplay Media

Add data-autoplay to automatically play audio/video when opened:

<lz-item 
    label="Podcast" 
    data-audio="episode.mp3"
    data-autoplay>
</lz-item>
